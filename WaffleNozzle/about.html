About the WaffleNozzleTM

The WaffleNozzle is a unique lid that screws onto diner-style squeeze bottles. It is perfect for family-owned restaurants looking an atmosphere that stands out from the corporate crowd. The WaffleNozzle uses an innovative, three-pronged approach to help customers spread their ketchup with ease. Your customers will enjoy focusing less on condiments and more on company. WaffleNozzle lids are sold seperatly from the bottle, making the WaffleNozzle ideal for lean shipping costs and a deal for businesses that already own squeeze bottles.
