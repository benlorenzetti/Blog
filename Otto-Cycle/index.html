Fundamental Limits of the Otto Cycle

There are fundamental, scientific limits to the efficiency of internal combustion engines (ICEs), which helps explain why electric drivetrains feel inevitable, and perhaps makes you empathetic to the plight of engineers in a culture that prides itself on craftmanship and performance of diesel drivetrains (Voltswagon).

While there is still room for ICE innovations, most of them are limited and based on materials science breakthroughs or using hybrid electric/mechanical drives to help tune power/efficiency performance tradeoffs.

I tried to show a simplified version of the thermodynamics in a speech. Even if you don't follow the math all the way (trying to read a speech powerpoint!), the general trends can still make you a more educated investor if you like car stocks.
