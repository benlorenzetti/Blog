Bit Parity

Dec. 14, 2017

Close your weary eyes child for you have seen
The muzzled casting off of Neutrality,
Shorn seems of society, split encodings of reality.

Rest now but raise a wary eye tonight
For sure as the sun, this cycle will fade,
And when she returns with the weather Gauge,—

Bear the marathon torch aloft from belfry‐arch,
Through concrete jungles and polluted webs, as a coded signal in flight,—
One if by land, and three if by sea;

For null light shines down across the shadow of state's boundary.
And two: drop your hands and hold fast sons of libery;
let two be the calling sign for a new bit parity.

Views expressed are those of Mr. Lorenzetti as an individual, not the Department of Defense.
