As I write this I have three homes: a metal cylinder under the ocean USS Florida, my family back in Ohio, and finally a more reclusive house with a wooded backyard in Georgia. But this split is temporary and financial independence is a part of the plan. Although you may have many moves, times away, and hours of work ahead, some investments are best to start today for independence tomorrow.
