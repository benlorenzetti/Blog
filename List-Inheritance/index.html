Is Inheritance Subtyping?

01 SEP 2017

Is inheritance equivalent to subtyping? If this question does not also afflict you with great anthropomorphic angst, then I seriously question your sanity. But fear not, you and I are not alone in our abject lack of understanding, objectively greater minds have thought about this before (some even went crazy, now raving lunacies about how objects are alive and we can pass them messages!). Luca Cardelli wrote one of the first papers (1) and Cook et al. added more in (2). And just because CS is prone to such things I included paper (3) by Cartwright and Abdel-Gawad.

  1. A Semantics of Multiple Inheritance
  2. Inheritance Is Not Subtyping.
  3. Inheritance Is Subtyping
