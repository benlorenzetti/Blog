Should I Buy or Rent?

Calculating fair market value for a home purchase or saying no and just renting could save you months of income. The following calculators are based on my first home purchase and how mortgage financing works, in particular the idea of a limiting dividend for 30 year mortgages and how it sets a lower bound on home prices.

The themes are simple. (1) Think like a landlord and tenant, not like a homeowner. (2) Understand the property price model. (3) Do the work to accurately estimate inputs but use tools to make comparisons easier.
