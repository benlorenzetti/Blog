Should I Buy or Rent?

Buying a home is a big deal and finding a competitive price could save you months of work. This series uses example values from my own first home purchase.

The themes are simple. (1) Think like a landlord and tenant, not like a homeowner. (2) Understand the property price model. (3) Do the work to accurately estimate inputs but use tools to make comparing offers easier.
