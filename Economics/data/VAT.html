It is pugilistic to analyze economics as a science rather than a liberal art, nevertheless as information has become evermore readily available, some data is required for a first punch.

There are three potential sources of wealth inequality: technological advancement, consolidation effects and structural inequalities. Wealth inequality due to technological advancement is best expressed by one's opinion on universal basic income; wealth inequality due to consolidation effects is best expressed by opinion on Capital in the 21st Century. But what are the effects of our federal reserve and federal tax scheme on wealth inequality in 2020?
